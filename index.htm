RATEMA

RAFIKI TEHAMA KWA MAENDELEO YA JAMII
(Friendly Techonologies For Community Development)

RATEMA is a Non Governmental Organization registered under Tanzanian laws Act No.24 of 2002 with registration No 0906. RATEMA is an acronym of Kiswahili terms "Rakifi Tehama kwa Maendeleo ya Jamii" which means a Friendly Technologies for Community Development.

RATEMA has realizing that there's a gap of insufficiently utilizing or using of technologies to make our communities more sustainable places to live, work and socialize in. RATEMA is focusing on eliminate or reduce this gap by creating open and innovative projects that can enrich communities and provide social support to the socities. With these projects created will also increase the level of understanding and awareness in the aspects of Health and Wellbeing, Social Development, Environmental and Educational

About RATEMA

RATEMA was formed in 2019 by a small group of youth, aiming at support the communities by co-create projects that can tackle social problems including promoting health and wellbeing, Social Development, Environmental and Educational.

VISSION

To see communities using Technologies in order to become more sustainable places to live, work and socialize in while building our national.

MISSION

Co-create open and innovative projects that can enrich local communities and provide social supports

Objectives

1. To empower our communities become more sustainable places to live, work and socialize in.

2. To advocate the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in our communities.

3. To use technologies in promoting health and wellbeing, support the most socially disadvantage members of the society.

4. To educate community especially young generation to make positive use of technologies in achieving one's goals for a brighter future.

5. To discourage enironmental harmful actions.
